Portfolio

Acutalize CRM

A part of a software development team working in an agile environment on a real CRM that Actualize uses to manage its prospective applicants. The system allows users to call and text leads through the Twilio API. Additionally, I worked on features involving its UI, including an internal restructuring that transforms the app into a Single Page Application, increasing ease of use. This app uses Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, and VueJS.

Odyssey Travel App

Odyssey: An app that I built, which assists travelers with finding locations and services, in cases of emergency and lack of availability, nearby their current location. This Ruby on Rails app was created using VueJS, Bootstrap, and multiple API requests from the Google Places API.
